ABOUT VERNON

Vernon Marcaida is a recent graduate of San Francisco State University as a major in Broadcast and Electronic Communivation Arts. Prior to attending San Francisco State, Vernon attended Skyline College, where he earned an Associate in Arts Degree for Transfer in Communication Studies , and Archbishop Riordan High School, where he earned his High School Diploma.

Vernon was born and raised in San Francisco by his mother, Connie, and Late father Verner, with his four siblings, Verner, Vanessa, Verne, and Vanah.

Vernon aspires to work video productions for the Golden State Warriors or the San Francisco Giants, and teach at a local college!
